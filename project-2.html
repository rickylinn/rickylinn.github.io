BCA ACADEMY (DCIM SYSTEM)

Redundancy Data Center Infrastructure Management (DCIM) system integrating both OT and IT environments for comprehensive monitoring and control. The solution includes energy tracking, asset management, and SS564 Green Mark report calculation to support sustainability compliance and efficient data center operations.
